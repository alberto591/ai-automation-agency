## Presentation Tips

### Before You Start:
- [ ] Test screen share quality
- [ ] Have demo environment ready (backup plan)
- [ ] Print cheat sheet for quick reference
- [ ] Have Calendly/signup links ready to paste in chat

### During Presentation:
- **Pace:** Spend 60% of time on slides 2, 4 (problem, ROI)
- **Engagement:** Ask questions after each slide
- **Eyes:** Look at camera when speaking, not slides
- **Energy:** Match their energy level (excited vs skeptical)

### After Presentation:
- **Immediate:** Send follow-up email with slides attached
- **Day 1:** Check if they opened the email (tracking)
- **Day 3:** Follow-up call or message if no response
- **Day 7:** Final urgency message ("last 3 pilot spots")
