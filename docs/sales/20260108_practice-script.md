## üé§ Recording Tips

**How to record yourself:**
1. Use Voice Memos app (iPhone) or Voice Recorder (Android)
2. Pretend your phone is the customer
3. Have full conversations (both sides)
4. Listen back with headphones
5. Cringe at first (normal!), improve each time

**What to listen for:**
- Pacing (too fast = nervous, too slow = boring)
- Filler words ("um", "uh", "like")
- Energy (sound excited! Not robotic)
- Clarity (are you mumbling?)
- Pauses (are you giving them space to talk?)
