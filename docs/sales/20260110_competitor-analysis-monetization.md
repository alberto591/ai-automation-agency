# Competitor Analysis & Monetization Strategy

**Date:** 2026-01-10
**Purpose:** Inform pricing, positioning, and go-to-market strategy

---

## Executive Summary

The real estate AI market is fragmented between **US leaders** (Ylopo, Structurely, Follow Up Boss) and **emerging Italian players** (Casavo, Keplero AI, Opsansa). Key insight: **No dominant Italian player** exists for WhatsApp-native lead qualification + property matching.

**Recommended Monetization:** Tiered subscription with per-agent pricing + usage add-ons.

## Part 2: Monetization Models in Market

### Model Comparison

| Model | Description | Used By | Pros | Cons |
|-------|-------------|---------|------|------|
| **Per-Agent/Seat** | €X/agent/month | Follow Up Boss, CRMs | Predictable, scales with team | Small agencies feel penalized |
| **Per-Lead Volume** | Tiered by # leads | Structurely | Fair usage-based | Complex to track |
| **Flat Subscription** | Fixed monthly fee | Basic tools | Simple pricing | Doesn't scale with value |
| **Usage-Based** | Per conversation/token | AI-native platforms | Pay-for-what-you-use | Unpredictable costs |
| **Commission-Based** | % of closed deals | Some lead sources | Aligned incentives | Hard to track, trust issues |
| **Outcome-Based** | Base + performance bonus | Emerging trend | High value alignment | Complex measurement |

## Part 3: Recommended Agenzia AI Pricing

### Pricing Philosophy
- **Simple to understand** (Italian agents are not tech-savvy)
- **Value-aligned** (more leads = more revenue for us)
- **Low barrier to entry** (no €1,500 setup fee)

### Proposed Tiers

| Tier | Price | Included | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| **Starter** | €199/mo | 1 agent, 100 leads/mo, WhatsApp only | Solo agents |
| **Professional** | €399/mo | 5 agents, 500 leads/mo, WhatsApp + Voice | Small agencies |
| **Enterprise** | €799/mo | Unlimited agents, unlimited leads | Large agencies |

### Add-Ons
| Feature | Price |
|---------|-------|
| Additional agent | €49/mo |
| AI property appraisals (Fifi) | €99/mo |
| Multi-language expansion | €49/mo |
| Priority support | €99/mo |

### Alternative: Per-Lead Model
| Volume | Price per Lead |
|--------|----------------|
| 0-100 leads/mo | €2.00/lead |
| 101-500 leads/mo | €1.50/lead |
| 500+ leads/mo | €1.00/lead |

## Key Takeaways

1. **No dominant Italian competitor** in WhatsApp + AI RE space
2. **Pricing sweet spot:** €199-399/mo for SMB agencies
3. **Avoid setup fees** - differentiator vs. US competitors
4. **Lead-based pricing** viable alternative for pay-per-performance positioning
5. **Commission-based model** too complex for initial launch
