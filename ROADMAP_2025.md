## üõ°Ô∏è Competitive Moat

### 1. Italian-First Advantage
- **Only AI** built for Italian cadastral law
- **Only AI** with OMI (Osservatorio del Mercato Immobiliare) data integration
- **Only AI** with EU AI Act compliance (Article 52 disclosure)
- **Only AI** with Italian legal disclaimers (Code of Professional Conduct)

### 2. WhatsApp-Native
- 11x engagement vs email (Housing.com case study)
- 98% open rate on WhatsApp vs 20% email
- Preferred channel for 70% of Italian customers (age 25-55)

### 3. Hybrid AI + Fifi Appraisal
- **No competitor** combines lead qualification + AI appraisal
- Fifi creates "stickiness" (agents can't switch to competitors)
- ML-based valuation = data moat (better with more data)

### 4. Hybrid Model (AI + Human Handoff)
- Agents trust it more (not "black box")
- Compliance-friendly (human review loop)
- Higher retention vs fully automated tools
